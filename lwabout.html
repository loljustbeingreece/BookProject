Meg March

Meg is the oldest and most traditionally feminine March sister. She acts as a second mother to her younger sisters.

"Meg shook her head, as she thought regretfully
of all the pretty things she wanted."

Jo March

Jo is the second eldest March sister. She is an amazing writer; some say say was better than Shakespeare. 

"Ill try and be what he loves to call me, 'a
little woman,' and not be rough and wild."

Beth March

Beth is the sweetest girl imaginable. She makes sure everybody, including her dolls, are loved and happy. 

"She resolved in her quiet little soul."

Amy March

Amy is a sympathetic and well-mannered girl. Her upper-class tastes refine her into an artistic lass.

"I am a selfish girl! But I'll truly try to be better."  

The March Family

None of us are perfect in any sense of the word. Through our daily lives, we are becoming better.
